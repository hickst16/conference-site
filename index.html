What is FoTI?

The Future of the Internet conference was established in 2003. Back then
it spoke about issues like the rising popularity of online shopping, WiFi,
and the rising superpower that was Google. Spearheaded by the top inventers,
engineers, commentators, and web designers in the technology industry,
this conference makes a goal to discuss and analyze the implications of the
Internet as we move forward. All angles and sides are covered including societal
and governmental. Additionally, everyday issues regarding the common person are
discussed since we live in an ambiguous time where anything can happen with such
rapidly progressing internet technology.
